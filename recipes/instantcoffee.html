Instant Coffee

Description

Instant coffee is a popular type of coffee that offers convenience and quick preparation.

It is commonly packaged in glass jars, plastic containers, or individual packets for single servings.

Ingredients

  • Instant coffee is made from coffee beans that have been roasted, ground, and brewed into a concentrated liquid form.
  • To make instant coffee, this liquid is then freeze-dried or spray-dried to remove the water content, resulting in granules or powder.

Steps

  1. Pour hot water over the coffee granules and stir.
  2. Enjoy.
