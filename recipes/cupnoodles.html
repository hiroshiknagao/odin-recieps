Cup Noodles

Description

Cup noodles, also known as instant noodles or ramen noodles in a cup, are a popular convenience food enjoyed by people worldwide.

Cup noodles typically come in a cylindrical container made of styrofoam or plastic.

Ingredients

  • The main components of cup noodles are pre-cooked noodles made from wheat flour, water, and salt.
  • Additionally, cup noodles contain a flavoring powder or seasoning sauce, which often includes ingredients like salt, soy sauce, dried vegetables, spices, and sometimes dehydrated meat or seafood.

Steps

  1. Go to a store.
  2. Find it. If not ask where it is politely
  3. Pay for it. Don't steal it.
  4. Go home. Boil the water. Pour hot water into the cup.
  5. Wait for three minutes and enjoy.
