Sushi

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice: A special short-grain rice seasoned with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt.
  • Nori (Seaweed).
  • Fish.
  • Wasabi.

Steps

  1. Prepare Sushi Rice.
  2. Prepare Ingerdients.
  3. Assembly.
  4. Rolling.
  5. Slicing.
  6. Serve and Enjoy.
